Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

What is ASAP (Amrita Students Assessment Platform)?
ASAP is an innovative platform designed to evaluate, educate, and develop students, enabling them to improve in competitive exams and placements. The platform offers comprehensive performance tracking across all semesters, personalized feedback, extra study sessions, and mock exams.
Who can use ASAP?
ASAP is intended for students, teachers, and administrators at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. Each user group has specific functionalities, such as performance tracking for students and session management for teachers.
How do I log in?
Students, teachers, and admins can log in using their college email and password through the login page. New users must first sign up and then proceed with login.

Performance and Evaluation

How does the Performance and Evaluation module work?
The Performance and Evaluation module enables teachers to upload marks, provide feedback, and generate reports. Students can access performance graphs and detailed feedback through their dashboard, allowing them to track their academic progress.
Can I view feedback on my performance?
Yes, students can view detailed feedback on their performance, including areas for improvement, by accessing their personalized dashboard within the Performance and Evaluation module.
What kind of reports are generated for students?
Teachers can generate graphical reports and detailed analysis of student performance, covering aspects such as subject-wise and semester-wise progress, which helps students understand their academic strengths and weaknesses.

Sessions and Training

How are sessions managed on ASAP?
Teachers can manage sessions by defining specific criteria such as the topic, number of students, and performance level. Notifications are sent to students about upcoming sessions, and the data is stored for future reference.
What is included in the Professional Training module?
The Professional Training module includes activities like group discussions, public speaking, and mock interviews to help students prepare for real-world challenges. Every student is ensured participation at least once per semester.
Can I view upcoming sessions?
Yes, students can view upcoming sessions and training opportunities from their dashboard, with session details such as date, topic, and participants clearly listed.

Technical Support

What should I do if I encounter technical issues?
If you experience any technical issues while using ASAP, you can contact the support team via email or the in-app chat. Ensure you provide a detailed description of the issue for a faster resolution.
How do I reset my password?
If you forget your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.
Who can I contact for help?
For any questions or concerns, you can contact the ASAP support team at support@asapplatform.com or use the Help section in your dashboard.
